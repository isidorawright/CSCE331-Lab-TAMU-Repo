Interests in Computer Science and Cybersecurity

Computer Engineering:

My major is Computer Engineering; a combination of both computer hardware and software. I find both the hardware and software aspects of computers to be very fascinating. The reason I chose this major is because of two reasons:

  1. My father was an Electrical Engineer. Therefore, I grew up doing various projects with him around the house (both electrical and non-electrical related). But I knew that I wanted to work with more than just the hardware of computers and other appliances.
  2. I took an AP coding course in high school and instantly fell in love with coding.

I put those two together and I found computer engineering.

My technical interest in this field is to work in making the synchronization between the hardware and software of a computer system run faster and smoother. I find how the hardware and software of an appliance communicate to each other to be able to run efficiently for users is very intriguing.

Cybersecurity:

My technical interests in this field are: incident response, pentesting, designing systems, etc. After having experience in non-technical assessments aspect of cybersecurity, I know that I want to work in the technical side of cybersecurity.
