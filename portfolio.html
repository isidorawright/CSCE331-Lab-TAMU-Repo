Internship:

Summer 2022:

This past summer, I did an internship with the DOE in Idaho Falls, Idaho through the OMNI program. My project was to write a gap analysis between NIST 800-53 revision 4 and revision 5. For those that do not know, NIST is a cybersecurity framework that organizations and businesses can use to help base their cybersecurity policies and procedures on. In 2020, NIST 800-53 revision 5 was released. My job was to see how rev 5 had been updated from rev 4, look at the DOE's policies and procedures, and write a report to tell them how their policies and procedures should be updated in order to be up to date with NIST 800-53 revision 5.

I was able to experience how to handle a real-time incident response situation.
